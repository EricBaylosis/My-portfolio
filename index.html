I am Eric Glenn D. Baylosis, a passionate and creative individual with a strong interest in web design and development. I have always been fascinated by the digital world and its endless possibilities. My journey into the realm of web design started as a hobby, but it quickly evolved into my true passion and career path.

I firmly believe that creativity knows no bounds, and the field of web design provides the perfect canvas to unleash my imagination. Taking up creative web design has allowed me to blend my technical skills with my artistic flair, giving me the freedom to create visually stunning and user-friendly websites.

Studying creative web design has been an exciting adventure, enabling me to dive into various aspects of the craft. From learning the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to exploring the principles of responsive design and user experience, every step of the learning process has been enriching and rewarding.
